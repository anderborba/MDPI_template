\section{Review Report}
\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=red!5!white,colframe=red!75!black,title=Review Report]
Dear authors, thank you for your manuscript. It concerns spatial filtering methods for SAR interferograms, trying to find a suitable filtering method with lower computing efforts.

The paper is well organized, well written and generally comprehensible.

I only ask the authors to make some changes in the paper title and abstract to stress out that it regards SPATIAL filtering for DInSAR/SBAS interferograms, as InSAR is a much wider term, in order not to be mistaken (as I was). Also InSAR noise can indicate something else.

In addition to these, I only have minor comments:

section 2.1.2-4 - please provide more details about the data: satellite/UAVSAR, band, maybe 2 dates if it is a satellite
figure 3 - please provide wavelength (band), Bperp, Btemp etc.
equation 13: what is F?

\end{tcolorbox}
